DAVID CALVO has been a professional woodcarver for forty-five years.

He was fortunate to be trained by professional European woodcarvers and sculptors. He has been a featured master woodcarver at many International Wood Working Trade Shows where he has given workshops on woodcarving technique and designing skills. David has also been a featured TV guest on the national PBS Television shows: "The Woodwright's Shop" with Roy Underhill and "The American Woodshop" with Scott Phillips. In addition, David writes articles for national publications to promote and share his skill. He is a sought-after teacher because his unique apprenticeship training is a rarity. In addition, he is also a natural speaker who has the ability to articulate the mechanics of the skill of wood carving in an easily understood fashion.
