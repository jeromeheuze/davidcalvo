The Fine Art of Wood Carving & Bronze Sculpture

North of Boston, Calvo Studio is dedicated to the fine art of hand carved woodcarving and sculpture. We design in both contemporary and classical styles. Our skills are varied: wood carved architectural ornament and decoration, hand-carved custom furniture ornament, carved ornamental pipe organ screens/shades, wood carved fireplace mantels and custom carved entry doors. The studio also does elegant hand carved lettering for dedication donor or commemorative plaques as well. Our Sculpture Studio also designs and creates figurative or abstract sculpture in wood or bronze and well as mixed medias. All work is custom to suit the client's need and we take pride in impeccable artistic penmanship of the highest caliber.

Wood Carving School • Woodcarving Workshops & Classes

Calvo Wood Carving School has been teaching students for thirty-five years with great success. Students have come from every state the country including Hawaii and Alaska as well as internationally from as far as South Korea to take classes with David Calvo. His training is unique and he teaches the fundamentals of woodcarving with a natural ability to articulate ideas in a clear and concise manner. He will show you a systematic new way to approach your wood carving projects. In addition, he trains you like an athlete by using muscle memory exercises for your hands in the same way a golfer trains his swing . David states with confidence, that he has refined his wood carving workshops to the point that they are like a "Shakespearean Play", meaning the workshops are completely laid out in a very calculated and succinct manner for the student's benefit and success within the five-day timeframe. Consider joining us to learn how to embrace your creativity and you'll enjoy yourself. The school also offers Woodworking Workshops, Spoon Making Classes as well as Blacksmithing Your Own Carving Tools. Click the woodcarving school tab in the index bar for more information.
